Bitcoin Checkout

Bitcoin Checkout provides an easy way to pay Bitcoin. You can pay through one of the supported bitcoin wallet companies, like Coinbase and Circle. With ShapeShift you can easialy convert you cryptocurrency of choice to bitcoin and send it directly to the receiver.

We hope to support other payment types in the direct future, like iDEAL and are open for suggestions and pull requests.

Warning this is an early beta, all usage at own risk.
